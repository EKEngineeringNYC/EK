Zone 01 · Company

One contractor. Every phase.

EK Engineering is a full-service fire alarm and life safety partner for New York City high-rise and commercial buildings. We handle the entire project lifecycle under one roof — so owners, developers, and property managers work with a single team from the first drawing to FDNY final sign-off.

Whether you are building new, renovating, or upgrading an existing system, we coordinate design, DOB filings, permits, electrical work, equipment procurement, panel programming, acceptance testing, and FDNY inspections with clear communication at every step.

Zone 02 · Services

Complete fire alarm scope

EK Engineering delivers every discipline required for a code-compliant, inspection-ready fire alarm system in NYC.

01

Design

Fire alarm layouts, riser diagrams, device schedules, and NFPA / NYC code-aligned system design.

02

File & permits

Plan filing, DOB submissions, permit applications, and documentation for agency review.

03

Electrical installation

Licensed electrical work — conduit, wiring, device mounting, FACP and NAC infrastructure.

04

Equipment supply

Fire alarm panels, detectors, notification appliances, power supplies, and accessories.

05

Program

Panel programming, zoning, cause-and-effect, elevator recall, and system integration.

06

Testing

Preliminary and acceptance testing, device verification, and inspection-ready documentation.

07

FDNY inspection

Coordination and support through FDNY field inspection and corrective actions if required.

08

Final sign-off

Project closeout, sign-off paperwork, and turnover of as-builts and O&M records.

Consultation

Expert guidance on code requirements, system upgrades, violations, Local Law compliance, and ongoing life safety strategy for your building.
